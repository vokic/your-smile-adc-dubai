7. FAQ – Cosmetic Dentistry in Dubai
Headline (H2): Frequently Asked Questions
Q1: What’s the difference between cosmetic and general dentistry?
General dentistry focuses on health and function, while cosmetic dentistry enhances appearance while often improving function too.
Q2: How long do cosmetic dental treatments last?
With proper care, veneers and crowns can last 10–15 years, implants can last a lifetime, and whitening can last 6–12 months with touch-ups.
Q3: Is cosmetic dentistry safe?
Yes. All treatments are carried out with biocompatible materials and advanced techniques, ensuring both safety and long-term success.
Q4: Who is a good candidate for cosmetic dentistry?
Anyone unhappy with the appearance of their smile,whether due to stains, chips, gaps, misalignment, or missing teeth can benefit. A consultation helps determine the right treatment plan.
Q5: Can I combine treatments for better results?
Yes. Many patients choose a combination, such as veneers with whitening, or implants with orthodontics, for a complete smile transformation.
