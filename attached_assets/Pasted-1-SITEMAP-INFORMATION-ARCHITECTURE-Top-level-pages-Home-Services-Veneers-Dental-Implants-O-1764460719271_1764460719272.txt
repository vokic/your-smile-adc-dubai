H2: Frequently Asked Questions About Veneers

Q1: How long do veneers last?
Porcelain veneers typically last 10‚Äì15 years, and composite veneers around 4‚Äì7 years. Their longevity depends on your oral hygiene, habits, and regular dental check-ups.

Q2: Will my veneers look natural?
Yes. Veneers are custom-made to match your tooth shape, size, and color. Porcelain veneers mimic natural enamel, giving a realistic, translucent appearance.

Q3: Is getting veneers painful?
The procedure is performed under local anesthesia and is usually pain-free. You might feel mild sensitivity for a few days, which quickly settles.

Q4: Porcelain vs composite veneers ‚Äì what‚Äôs the difference?
Porcelain is more durable, stain-resistant, and natural-looking; composite is more affordable and faster but may require touch-ups sooner. We‚Äôll recommend the best option based on your goals and budget.

Q5: Do veneers damage my natural teeth?
We remove only a very thin layer of enamel to create space and ensure a perfect fit. When done by experienced dentists, veneers do not harm your dental health.
